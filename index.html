Welcome to an experiment in co-working - replicating the comradery and feel of an open office.

Increase productivity without opening slack, or setting up meetings and agendas.

How it works

  • Work in 40 min intervals with 15 min breaks
  • Timer on screen share and status in chat
  • Room is generaly open 9-12 and 1-5 EST
  • Join or leave whenever you want
  • Talk or mute whenever you want

Thanks for checking out this experiment, Send suggestions to max@duggan.me
